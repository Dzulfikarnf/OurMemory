And We Survive

We didn’t come out unscarred,
but we came out together.
Still standing,
still choosing each other.

After the Storm

We went through quiet days, heavy talks, and moments that almost pulled us apart. Not everything was solved perfectly, but we learned how to stay. How to listen, how to wait, and how to hold on without forcing. This part isn’t about winning or losing, it’s about surviving together, and choosing to keep going slowly, honestly, as us.

Learning to Stay

After everything we went through, we learned how to stay without hurting. We talked more carefully, listened more honestly, and gave each other space when it was needed. It wasn’t about fixing everything at once, but about growing together, slowly, quietly, and with intention.

Quietly Together

There was no big moment here, no celebration or loud happiness. Just us, being together in silence that finally felt safe. We didn’t need to explain much anymore. Being side by side was enough, and for the first time in a while, the quiet didn’t hurt.

Still Choosing You

After all the noise, the silence, and the growing pains, we’re still here. Not because everything is perfect, but because we keep choosing each other. Again and again, in small ways that matter. This is us, and this is where we stay.

One More Time

Every memory here is a small part of our story. If you want, you can start again from the very beginning.
