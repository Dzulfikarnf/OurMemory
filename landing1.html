Happiest Day

Not perfect, not loud
just a day that felt right.

The Moment It Felt Right

That day didn’t come with big plans or grand moments. We just spent time together, without rushing or overthinking anything. Everything felt lighter, calmer, and easier than before. It wasn’t about doing something special, it was about how natural it felt to be there, together, and finally at ease.

Timezone Day

That day, we spent our time at Timezone, playing games without really caring about who won. We laughed over small victories, failed miserably at some games, and tried again anyway. It felt light, silly, and fun, like we were just two people enjoying the moment, forgetting about everything else for a while.

Hayabusa & Kagura

That day, we brought home two tiny lives and gave them names that somehow felt just right, Hayabusa and Kagura. Watching them move around, eat, and explore their little world made us smile for no big reason. It was a small moment, but it felt warm, like we were building something together, even in the simplest way.

Photobooth in Braga

We stepped into a small photobooth in Braga, not expecting much, just laughs and random poses. The camera flashed, time paused for a moment, and somehow those simple frames captured something real, our smiles, our closeness, and a happiness that didn’t need to be explained. It’s the kind of memory you keep without realizing how precious it’ll become.
