First Met

The day our story quietly began.
No plans, no expectations, just us.

The Beginning

We didn’t think much of that day at first. It felt ordinary, simple, but unforgettable. But somehow, that moment became the start of everything that followed.

When It Was Still Messy

Those were our early days, when emotions ran high and everything felt a little messy. We didn’t always know how to communicate, how to react, or how to understand each other properly. Mistakes happened, feelings clashed, and nothing felt steady yet. But even in that fragile beginning, we were already trying, learning as we went, and slowly figuring out what it meant to choose each other.

Learning to Walk Together

This was the phase when we started moving forward, even though nothing felt fully right yet. We were still carrying doubts, misunderstandings, and unfinished emotions from the beginning. Some days felt heavy, some steps felt unsure, but we kept walking anyway. Slowly, we learned that being together wasn’t about having everything figured out, it was about choosing to stay and grow, even when things were still fragile.

Getting Used to Us

This was when things started to feel more familiar, even though we were still at the beginning. We began to understand each other’s habits, moods, and little patterns. It wasn’t perfect, and we still stumbled sometimes, but being together no longer felt strange. Slowly, us became something we were getting used to, gentle, growing, and quietly meaningful.

One More Time

Every memory here is a small part of our story. If you want, you can start again from the very beginning.
